Who We Are, What We Do

Who We Are, What We Do

Baobab Travel tailors personally guided tours with a tour guide who has lived in Malawi for over 30 years. We take the worry and stress of navigating through car hire and African road travel by escorting you in our comfortable 4×4 SUV.

Baobab Travel offers guided and self-drive tours throughout Malawi and into Zambia, and services include personalised itinerary planning suited to your interests and budget.

Alan Grimes, owner of Baobab Travel and Malawi enthusiast looks forward to showing you the warm heart of Africa and its stunning landscapes. In addition to Lake Malawi where he is happiest snorkeling, kayaking and fishing, he has excellent local knowledge of the flora and fauna in the various wildlife reserves and is a keen birder and wildlife photographer.

Tours generally include land and lake activities and a personalized itinerary will guarantee an excellent, stress-free experience in Malawi. We invite you to explore our website and visit the Raves and Reviews link at the bottom of the page for feedback from some of Baobab Travel’s satisfied guests. Please contact us at alan@baobabtravel.net or through our Facebook page or Twitter. We will be happy to discuss travel opportunities around Malawi and answer any questions about our Company.
